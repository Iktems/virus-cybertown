The game is simple, the virus has to eliminate the digital professions and the digital professions have to eliminate the virus. Each night the virus can eliminate a job and they try to hide their identity

In Cybertown, some digital professionals were attacked by viruses. Each night, attacks are committed. The professionals have to join their forces to try to eliminate the viruses before the town loses all the professionals.

- The players appoint or draw lots for a Community Manager, who does not play the game but directs it.
- The Community Manager gives each player 1 character card face down. Each player looks discreetly at his job, then places his card face down in front of him.
- The Community Manager puts the citizens to sleep. He says: "It's night, you can close your eyes".
- All the players bow their heads and close their eyes.
- Then, depending on the choice of characters in the game, the Community manager calls up the various jobs.
- All the actions need to be validate by a right answer of the quiz.

- The citizens before to be eliminate can respond to a quiz to switch or not in the enemy camp during the night.
- Before using a power or before eliminate a person, the citizens must respond to a quiz.
- If someone die, the narrator takes his card and read the description of the role.
